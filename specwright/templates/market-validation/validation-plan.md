## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|-------------|--------|---------------------|
| Low conversion rate (<3%) | Medium | High | Test 3+ headline variants, optimize CTA placement |
| High CPA (>€15) | Medium | Medium | Refine targeting, pause low-performing ads, lower bids |
| Insufficient traffic (<500 visitors) | Low | High | Increase budget, expand targeting, extend timeline |
| Technical issues (page doesn't load) | Low | High | Test deployment on multiple devices before launch |
| No email signups (0% conversion) | Low | Critical | Immediate headline change, add social proof, simplify form |

### Contingency Plans

**If Conversion Rate <2% after Week 1**:
- Pause campaigns immediately
- Conduct 5 user tests (ask users to review landing page)
- Rewrite headline based on feedback
- Re-launch Week 2

**If CPA >€20 after Week 1**:
- Pause worst-performing 50% of ads
- Reallocate budget to top performers
- Narrow targeting (focus on best-converting segment)

**If Total Traffic <200 after Week 1**:
- Increase daily budget by 50%
- Expand targeting (broader interests, wider age range)
- Add retargeting campaign

- **Tracking**: Google Analytics conversion goal

- **Target**: ≤ €[X]
- **Measurement**: Total ad spend ÷ Email signups

**Total Addressable Market (TAM)**:
- **Target**: ≥ [X] users
- **Measurement**: Research-based estimation from market data
- **Source**: @competitor-analysis.md, industry reports

### Secondary Metrics (Optimization Indicators)

- **Bounce Rate**: Target <60% (% who leave immediately)
- **Avg Time on Page**: Target >1 minute (indicates engagement)
- **CTA Click Rate**: Target >10% (% who click button before form)
- **Form Start Rate**: Target >50% of CTA clickers (how many start filling form)
- **Form Completion Rate**: Target >80% of starters (how many finish)
