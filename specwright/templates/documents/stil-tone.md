## Writing Style

### Sentence Structure

| Guideline | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **Keep it short** | Max 20 words per sentence |
| **Active voice** | "We send reminders" not "Reminders are sent" |
| **Start with action** | "Create invoices in 60 seconds" |
| **One idea per sentence** | Don't chain multiple thoughts |

### Paragraph Style

- **Length:** 2-4 sentences max
- **Structure:** Lead with the key point
- **Flow:** Short → Medium → Short rhythm
- **Spacing:** Generous white space
